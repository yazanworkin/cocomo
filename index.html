Accuracy Evaluation — MRE & MMRE

Compare estimated effort against actual effort to compute MRE (Magnitude of Relative Error) and classify accuracy. Enter multiple data points for MMRE (Mean MRE).

MRE = |Actual − Estimated| ÷ Actual   |   MMRE = Σ MRE ÷ N   |   PRED(25) = % of MRE ≤ 0.25
